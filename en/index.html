Founder

A Different Vision

André da Silva

Founder & Director LinkedIn

An educational project based on the ideas of Marvin Minsky and Seymour Papert, as well as quality knowledge from computer science, neuroscience, psychology and sociology, for a radically modern education, no nonsense, that optimizes itself through good technologies and good human relationships.

To reach the top of each person's potential in a highly pleasurable way with a sense of meaning. Oscillating between moments of deep productivity and moments of relaxed divergence.

The above-average future economic and social value developed in each person becomes a natural consequence. Focused not only on curriculum excellence but on interesting skills beyond it.

6. PARENTS

Specialized Consulting for Parents
+

The problem we know well

Grades in free fall. A lack of motivation no one seems able to fix. Diagnoses that frighten more than they help. The feeling that school isn't doing enough, that the psychologist keeps saying the same things, that the doctor has no time to explain.

If you've found your way here, you've probably already tried several paths. We know this situation well — and we know what's missing.

What we do

Direct consulting with André da Silva, McMinsky's founder, with doctoral training in learning neuroscience and over a decade of intensive hands-on experience with challenging youth and their parents.

  • Deep analysis of the academic, behavioural and family situation
  • Concrete, personalized action plan — not generic advice
  • Neuroscience-based strategies that actually work in practice
  • Guidance on the education system and the best available options
  • Understanding your child's brain and profile — how they think, learn and what blocks them
  • Approaches and techniques most professionals don't know or don't apply
  • Support for handling difficult everyday situations at home
  • Practical resources you can use immediately

Who it's for

  • Parents of children or teens with terrible school results
  • Situations that need an urgent, competent plan
  • Neurodivergent children and teens (ADHD, dyslexia, autism, giftedness, etc.)
  • Chronic lack of motivation or school refusal
  • Difficult behaviour at home or at school
  • Parents who want to better understand what's happening and what they can do differently
  • Anyone who needs an experienced, honest second opinion

What it is NOT

This is not a medical, psychological or psychiatric consultation. We do not replace those professionals — we complement them. We are the practical link that's missing between what school doesn't solve, what the psychologist has no time to teach and what nobody explains to parents.

Why McMinsky

Over a decade in the field, with hundreds of young people and families. Advanced training in neuroscience. Deep knowledge of education systems. Real — not theoretical — experience with the most challenging cases. And above all, results.

There is hope. And there is method.
